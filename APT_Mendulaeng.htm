Apartments Mendula

Pula Istria Croatia

Dear guests

Welcome

The **Mendula** Apartments are located in a quiet part of Pula, at Bunarska cesta 13, offering the perfect combination of comfort, privacy, and authentic Mediterranean charm.

Set within a spacious private courtyard, the property offers parking spaces under video surveillance, swings, a comfortable outdoor wooden seating area, and a barbecue – an ideal place for relaxing and spending time outdoors. The warm, family-friendly atmosphere of the property makes every guest feel at home.

Two modernly furnished 55 m² apartments are available, perfect for 2 to 5 people. Comfortable, fully equipped, and surrounded by greenery, they are ideal for families, couples, or friends who want a carefree and relaxing holiday.

Among fig trees, olive trees, and Mediterranean herbs, the Mendula Apartments offer a peaceful natural environment that invites you to unwind and recharge. If you wish to escape the stress of everyday life and enjoy the tranquility of nature we are at your service.

With prior notice, we are happy to welcome your dog as well, provided that its behavior does not disturb other guests.

Guests are provided with bed linen and a set of towels per person (with replacement if needed), as well as basic hygiene supplies and cleaning products.

Free Wi-Fi is available in both the apartments and the courtyard, and the TV offers Croatian and Italian terrestrial channels as well as a wide selection of international satellite stations. Bicycles are also often available in the courtyard and can be used free of charge.

The host will personally welcome you, assist with your accommodation, and provide all necessary information about your stay, nearby amenities, and local experiences – ensuring a warm, family-style hospitality throughout your visit.
Contact us for your reservation.

Apartment Sunrise

Apartment Sunrise consists of three rooms: living room with kitchen, bedroom, bathrooms and terraces.

In the kitchen, you have at your disposal an induction plate, a hood, refrigerator with freezer, dishwasher, oven, microwave oven, filter and espresso coffee machines, mocha, kettle, toaster, stick blender, set of utensils and dishes for preparing and serving food...

The living room is equipped with a TV, air conditioning and a sofa bed.

The bathroom has a shower cabin, toilet bowl, bidet, washing machine and hair dryer.

The bedroom has a spacious wardrobe, double bed, air conditioning, TV, iron, and extra bed.

Surroundings

The house is set within a beautifully landscaped yard surrounded by greenery, creating the perfect environment for relaxation, play, and carefree moments outdoors.

The garden features fig and olive trees, a grapevine-covered pergola, a small vegetable garden, and plenty of flowers and Mediterranean herbs such as rosemary, lavender, sage, basil, and bay leaf. In the shade of the olive tree, you will find a large wooden table with benches – ideal for gatherings – as well as a barbecue for preparing delicious meals.

The house is located at the very end of a quiet street, and behind it begins a forest path – perfect for walks, jogging, or strolling with your dog.

Pula - Istria

The Pula coastline is full of beautiful pebble and rocky coves and beaches, where you can enjoy the crystal-clear sea.

Choose a well-maintained beaches with numerous sports and entertainment facilities at Hidrobaza or Verudela, or peaceful, untouched beaches far from the crowds, such as Proština Beach and the surrounding area,or the beaches in the slightly more distant Kamenjak nature park.

In addition to its beautiful beaches, Pula also has a very rich history.

Inhabited for more than 3,000 years, it offers a wealth of historical monuments from the Roman period, such as the Amphitheatre, the Temple of Augustus, and the Arch of the Sergii, as well as examples of Byzantine architecture like the Church of St. Mary Formosa, and numerous Austro-Hungarian fortresses.

Pula is located on the Istrian peninsula, which offers many idyllic places to visit.

Location

The Mendula Apartments are located in a quiet neighborhood at the very entrance to Pula, at Bunarska cesta 13, just 150 meters from the main road leading to and from the city center.

A grocery store, pharmacy, gas station, ATM, restaurants, cafés, and the bus stop are 250–300 meters away, while the area with supermarkets (Konzum, Plodine, Kaufland, Lidl, Pevex) is 3 km away — as is Pula’s historic center (the Arena, Kaštel fortress, Forum, Arch of the Sergii, and more).

Surrounded by greenery and quiet streets, the location of our apartments offers easy access to everything Pula has to offer — from historical landmarks and cultural attractions to a variety of beaches to suit every taste.
