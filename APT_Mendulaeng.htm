Pula – Istria

The Pula coastline is rich in beautiful pebble and rocky bays and beaches with crystal-clear sea.
Choose organized beaches with sports and entertainment facilities such as Hidrobaza or Verudela, or quiet untouched beaches away from crowds, such as Proština or the nearby protected nature park Cape Kamenjak.

In addition to its beaches, Pula boasts a rich history. Inhabited for more than 3,000 years, it offers numerous historical monuments from Roman times, including the Amphitheatre, Temple of Augustus, Arch of the Sergii, as well as Byzantine and Austro-Hungarian heritage.

Pula is located in the south of the Istrian peninsula, which offers many idyllic places to explore.

Location

Mendula Apartments are located at the very entrance to Pula, 150 m from the main road leading to and from the city center.

A grocery store, pharmacy, gas station, ATM, restaurants, cafe and bus stop are 250-300 meters away,
the part of the city with supermarkets (Konzum, Plodine, Kaufland, Lidl, Pevex) is 3 km away, as is the historic center of Pula (Arena, Kaštel, Forum, Arch of the Sergi...).

The most beautiful family, secluded or dog-friendly beaches are 3-4 km away

Dear guests

Apartments Mendula are located in a quiet part of Pula, offering a perfect combination of comfort and authentic atmosphere.

The spacious garden includes video-surveilled parking spaces.

We offer two modern, fully equipped apartments of 55 m², accommodating 2 to 5 guests.

Surrounded by fig trees, olive trees, and Mediterranean greenery, Apartments Mendula provide a peaceful environment for a carefree and relaxing holiday.

Upon prior notice, we are happy to welcome your dog, provided it does not disturb other guests.

Bed linen and a towel set per person are provided (replacement available on request), along with basic toiletries and cleaning supplies.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the apartments and yard. TV offers Croatian and Italian channels, as well as a wide selection of satellite channels. Bicycles are often available for free use.

Your host will personally welcome you, assist with accommodation, and provide all necessary information about your stay, nearby facilities, and local experiences — ensuring a warm, family-friendly atmosphere.

Guests can enter the apartment from 2 pm or by appointment, while departure is scheduled for 10 am or by appointment.

Contact us for reservations.
