Apartments Mendula

Pula Istria Croatia

The Mendula apartments are located in a family house situated right next to a forest, in a quiet part of Pula, 3 km from the city center.

Our property offers two modernly equipped apartments, each accommodating from 2 up to a maximum of 5 people. They provide comfortable accommodation in a peaceful environment, ensuring a completely undisturbed holiday. They are ideal for family stays.

Man’s best friend (dog) is also welcome,, with prior notice and if his behavior does not disturb other guests.

There is a parking area with video surveillance in the courtyard.

Bed linen and towels are provided. Wi-Fi is available in the apartments and in the courtyard. On the TV you can watch Croatian and Italian channels, and via satellite other channels.

Dear guests

House rules

The host will welcome the guests and provide all necessary assistance regarding their accommodation. The host will also provide any information the guests need or request, which the host can provide.

It is recommended to close the main entrance after midnight.

Please turn off the air conditioner when leaving the apartment.

Use of the swing, bicycles, barbecue... is at your own risk.

Please avoid making noise that could disturb other guests, especially between 10:00 p.m. and 8:00 a.m.

Bringing people who are not guests is not allowed without the host’s consent.

Items from the apartment inventory may not be taken outside the apartment.

Smoking is not allowed inside the apartment, but it is permitted on the terrace.

Please do not change the boiler settings.

Dispose of waste as follows: paper in the blue container; bottles, plastic bottles, and cans in the labeled green container; other plastic and metal waste in the yellow container; and unsorted waste in the unlabeled green container, in closed bags.

Check-in is possible after 2:00 p.m. or by prior arrangement. On the day of departure, we kindly ask guests to leave the apartment by 10:00 a.m. or as agreed with the host, and to invite the host for an apartment inspection and key handover.

The host is obliged to keep any found or forgotten items for at least 6 months, or handle them in accordance with an agreement with the guest.

Apartment Sunrise

Apartment Sunrise consists of three rooms: living room with kitchen, bedroom, bathrooms and terraces.

In the kitchen, you have at your disposal an induction plate, a hood, refrigerator with freezer, dishwasher, oven, microwave oven, filter and espresso coffee machines, mocha, kettle, toaster and a set of utensils and dishes for preparing and serving food.

The living room is equipped with a TV, air conditioning and a sofa bed.

The bathroom has a shower cabin, toilet bowl, bidet, washing machine and hair dryer.

The bedroom has a spacious wardrobe, double bed, air conditioning, TV, iron, and extra bed.

Pula - Istria

The Pula coastline is full of beautiful pebble and rocky coves and beaches, where you can enjoy the crystal-clear sea.

Choose a well-maintained beaches with numerous sports and entertainment facilities at Hidrobaza or Verudela, or peaceful, untouched beaches far from the crowds, such as Proština Beach and the surrounding area,or the beaches in the slightly more distant Kamenjak nature park.

In addition to its beautiful beaches, Pula also has a very rich history.

Inhabited for more than 3,000 years, it offers a wealth of historical monuments from the Roman period, such as the Amphitheatre, the Temple of Augustus, and the Arch of the Sergii, as well as examples of Byzantine architecture like the Church of St. Mary Formosa, and numerous Austro-Hungarian fortresses.

Pula is located on the Istrian peninsula, which offers many idyllic places to visit.

Location

The Mendula Apartments are located in a quiet neighborhood at the very entrance to Pula, at Bunarska cesta 13, just 150 meters from the main road leading to and from the city center.

A grocery store, pharmacy, gas station, ATM, restaurants, cafés, and the bus stop are 250–300 meters away, while the area with supermarkets (Konzum, Plodine, Kaufland, Lidl, Pevex) is 3 km away — as is Pula’s historic center (the Arena, Kaštel fortress, Forum, Arch of the Sergii, and more).

Surrounded by greenery and quiet streets, the location of our apartments offers easy access to everything Pula has to offer — from historical landmarks and cultural attractions to a variety of beaches to suit every taste.
